About:

Drawing Paths:

draw lines between the clicks on canvas

New Path:

lines will continue to connect as you click until you click this

Width:

set width of lines (1-99)

Cap:

choose whether lines in your path will stop immediately, be rounded, or be squared

Shading: Colors:

set color of shapes and lines

Opacity:

set opacity of shapes and lines (0-99)

Shapes:

click "draw shape" and draw lines as if drawing a path, and "end shape" to fill it with your color!

Curves:

choose whether you'd like the shape to connect your points with quadratic/cubic curves (or just basic linear edges)

Circle:

bit different.. just click a point on your canvas as the center and click again to set the radius

Erasers: Erase Last:

remove most recently added shape/line from your canvas

Clear:

removes everything you've created from the canvas (except for an image just in case you want to keep the background)

Images: Add Image:

choose an image file to add to your canvas

Erase Image:

just in case you do not want to keep the background after all
